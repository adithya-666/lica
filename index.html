Our System

Lica Main Module for Laboratory System

Pre Analytics

Administration and Management of patient examinations in the Hospital involve several crucial steps. These steps include Patient data entry, Check-In updates, Blood sample collection, Sample labeling, Barcode printing, and the input of examination tool data.

Analytics

The process of blood examination involves the integration between the LICA information system and the blood examination analyzer, with the recording of the processing time. The completed blood examination results can be verified by analysts and validated by doctors, with the recording of the validation time.

Post Analytics

After completing patient examination tests, important steps to be taken include reviewing the test results, conducting final validation, and printing a summary of the test results.
